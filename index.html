Welcome to Good Grub!

Tired of hoity-toity restaurants that charge a fortune for tiny dishes? At Good Grub we believe in serving good, hearty meals to good honest folks at good, honest prices. You will not leave hungry! (Or bankrupted!)

Dig in to heart-warming, belly-filling dishes made with love by our team of hard-working chefs. If you compliment head chef John, he might even invite you out for a pint!

And, if you still have any space left, why not treat yourself to one of our delicious deserts? We find that stomachs become miraculously empty when people set eyes on our Death-by-Chocolate cake.
